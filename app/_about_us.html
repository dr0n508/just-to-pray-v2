About Us

Just Pray It™ was created to help unlock the power of prayer for every need no matter where you are. You’ll now have free access to resources on this website to help you find God’s provision for all things. By joining our community as a Prayer Member, we pray for you, pray with you, and you can pray for others in need. When you join, you will be connecting with like-minded believers praying in agreement with God’s unwavering love. You will also find a wide selection of recorded prayers, music and videos with words of love, care, hope and inspiration. We’ll also send you information on urgent needs throughout the world and share upcoming special prayer events. Just Pray It is your online resource to pray and reflect on God’s goodness and blessing in your life.
